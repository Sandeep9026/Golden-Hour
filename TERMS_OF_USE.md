# Terms of Use

## Golden Hour

By using Golden Hour, you acknowledge that:

- the app is an emergency coordination aid
- emergency response availability cannot be guaranteed
- location-based features depend on device permissions, connectivity, and service availability
- users must still contact official emergency services when necessary

## Acceptable Use

Users should not:

- submit fake emergencies
- misuse responder workflows
- enter misleading location or profile data intentionally
- abuse communication or support channels

## Service Scope

Golden Hour may provide:

- incident reporting
- responder coordination support
- incident tracking
- notification and preference workflows

Golden Hour does not guarantee:

- medical diagnosis
- ambulance dispatch
- emergency-service acceptance or arrival time

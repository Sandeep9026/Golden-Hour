# Privacy Policy

## Golden Hour

Golden Hour collects limited account and safety information to support emergency coordination features.

## Data We May Store

- Name
- Phone number
- Role selection
- Training status
- Vehicle number
- GPS coordinates used for incidents and nearby alert logic
- Notification and device settings
- Emergency contacts added by the user
- Support and feedback submissions

## Why This Data Is Used

- To identify users as drivers, responders, or dispatchers
- To support incident reporting and responder matching
- To display nearby incident information
- To maintain settings, notification state, and emergency contact workflows
- To improve the service through support requests and issue reports

## Emergency Disclaimer

Golden Hour is intended as an emergency coordination aid. It is not a guaranteed substitute for official emergency services or professional medical care.

## User Control

Users may update profile data, settings, and emergency contacts through the app. Data deletion and account handling should be supported through future production admin workflows.
